Designed, devloped, and performed an experiment to analyze the contents of a specifc Vitamin in a multi-vitamin chewable gummy.
The chosen vitamin was Ascrbic Acid, vitamin C. The chewable gummy is dissovlable in water, however, the gelatin and impurities in the gummy are also dissolveable in water. 
This presented an interesting challenge to overcome when looking to extract the organic contents in the sample. 
A 50:50 mix of Methanol and Water, with heat, was used to dissolve the gummy, then extract all of the organic contents from it, as the gelatin and most impurites were non-organic.
This created an organic solution that was ready to be extracted, and analyzed in the High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) instrument.
This process was repeated for the pure vitamin C gummy, and the results were compared.
With a percent error of 34.5, the results are well within experimental range, considering the numerous imourties that could not be extracted during the experiment.
The overall goal to qualty control check the claimed vitamin was accomplished.
